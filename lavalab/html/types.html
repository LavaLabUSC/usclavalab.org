designer

Passionate about UX and Design Thinking? Know the fundamentals of AI/PS? We'd love to bring you onto a team to design both software and hardware. Grow your portfolio and sharpen your design senses.

developer

Swift? NodeJS? Django? Arduino? Makerbot? We love it all. If you've talent in building great hardware or software projects, apply to be a developer. You'll perfect your skills with real-world experience.

Our product members bring together the rest of the team: they create marketing campaigns, plan user acquisition, and perfect financial reports. Product members help push the project into a functioning business as the semester-long curriculum culminates.
